Предложение - Sentence

1. There are the simple (one subject and one verb) and complex (several subjects and several verbs) sentences. This is a simplified explanation, but it is enough for a beginner.

2. There are two types of the simple sentences:

One-part sentence - usually you find them in the literature. Two-part sentence - the most important for every day use
Вечер (subject). (It is evening.) Папа (subject) побледнел (verb). (The dad turned pale.)
Дождь (subject). (It is raining. / It rains.) Идет (verb) дождь (subject). (It is raining. / It rains.)

3. The two-part sentence include: subject + verb.
Я иду на прогулку. [Subject: pronoun + verb + preposition + noun]. (I am going for a walk.)
Марта любит меня. [Subject: noun + verb + personal pronoun]. (Marta loves me.)
Мы поедем в Италию следующим летом. [Subject: pronoun + verb + preposition + noun + adjective + noun]. (We are going to Italy next summer.)
У меня есть велосипед. [Preposition + subject: pronoun + verb + noun]. (I have a bicycle)
Мне нравится читать долгими зимними вечерами. [Subject: pronoun + verb + verb + adjective + noun]. (I like reading for the long winter evenings.)
